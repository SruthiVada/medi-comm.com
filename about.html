Our Story

Our goal is to provide reliable healthcare service for our community during these unprecedented times. Before the pandemic, we were an in-person clinic. Once COVID-19 started to affect our communities, we transitioned online to protect the health of both our staff and patients. It is our mission to always provide quality healthcare to our patients.

In 1992 we opened our first clinic in Seattle

In 2005 we expanded our clinics to 30
new locations across the country

In 2010 we transitioned into a hybrid clinic
offering our services both online and in person
